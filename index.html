Dashain Festival

Celebration of Victory and Blessings

About Dashain

Dashain is the biggest and most auspicious festival celebrated in Nepal. It symbolizes the victory of good over evil. It usually falls in September or October and lasts for 15 days.

Main Days: Ghatasthapana, Fulpati, Maha Ashtami, Maha Navami, Vijaya Dashami, Kojagrat Purnima.

Traditions

Families reunite during Dashain. Elders give tika and jamara to the younger members, blessing them with prosperity and longevity. People also fly kites, play swings, and enjoy feasts.
